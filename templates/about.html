About Dark Forest Interactive

Dark Forest Interactive is an experimental project that showcases the creative potential of artificial intelligence in storytelling. This entire experience, from the story to the visuals and voices, has been created using various AI technologies working together.

AI Technologies Used

  • ChatGPT: Creates all story content, character interactions, and transmissions
  • ElevenLabs: Generates the realistic voice transmissions you hear
  • Midjourney & Runway: Creates all the visual elements and artwork

Project Features

  • Immersive Storytelling: A deep space narrative that evolves through AI-generated transmissions every 20 minutes
  • Voice Synthesis: Realistic AI-generated character voices
  • Dynamic Content: Story adapts and changes based on various factors
  • Social Integration: Automated updates across platforms

How It Works

  • New transmissions are generated every 20 minutes
  • Each transmission is uniquely created by AI
  • Voice synthesis converts text to realistic speech
  • Story evolves based on internal narrative logic

System Note

This is an experimental project that demonstrates how multiple AI systems can work together to create an engaging narrative experience. All transmissions, voices, and visuals are AI-generated to simulate deep space communications.
